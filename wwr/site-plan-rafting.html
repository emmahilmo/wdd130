Overview

Purpose

Our purpose here at Dry Oar Rafting is to help you spend time with family and friends out in nature and have a unique experience that you will remember.

Audience

Water rafting is a low cost activity that almost anyone one can participate in. We have beginner courses for the minimum age of 7 years old, for intermediate course, the minimum age of 14 years old. There are rafting courses for different age ranges and levels of skill. We will help you find the right course for you.
